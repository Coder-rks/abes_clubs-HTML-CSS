ABOUT US



Well said by socialist
'Sean o casey'

"All the world is stage and most of us are despirately unrehearsed". Dramatics is an art which is world wide famous and is performed almost in all countries of the world. Basically it is a reflection of Society,Cultures,Customs,Traditions etc. through acting on the stage. Dramatics is a meditation it teaches you to be self controlled, disciplined, more presentative , stage lover, patient etc. It teaches to play different different roles in life,to handle different situations.

Dramatics club is the one of the largest club of ABES ENGINEERING COLLEGE and has more than 100 registered members. It has successfully left its mark from college to national level. Stage shows , Nukkad Nataks, Mono Acts, Promotional Acts all have been the part of dramatics club achievment till date.
