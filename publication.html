ABOUT US



This club is responsible for publishing monthly "NEWSLETTER" & quarterly "EDGE".

The Newsletter is meant for circulating the information among students regarding events in the previous months and upcoming events also. It is also the medium for the office bearers of the college to let the students know about the new or revised rules or regulations of the college or UPTU.

Edge is the means to showcase the achievements of the college, faculty and students among other colleges.
