ABOUT US

Embedded Systems & Robotics Club, ABES Engineering College Ghaziabad aims to impart collective learning to the yearning students & provide them a platform to transfigure their learning to build something new that might automates/controls/monitors or commands something.

The club its session 2014-15 with a workshop on Smart Systems with a participation of 114 students. Since then, it has got a strength of 140+ students with a major strength of around 80 students from ECE Deptt. We also have a lab setup (eYantra Lab Setup) in the campus itself to learn and explore the topic. We have been conducting various workshops & seminars on Smart Systems, Image Processing, PCB Designs etc during the past semester. Besides, we conduct a 2 Hr seminar each Friday on various technologies referring to this field. Robocon 2015 and eYantra Robotics Competition 2015 are two competitions that the club is targeting for under Mr. Ajit Shukla (EDC Head) & Mr. Vivek Kr Verma (Club Counselor) in the March 2015. Two teams are participating in eYantra Robotics Competition 2015 with their leaders as Mohit garg and Abhishek Shukla. A team of Faculty (Names) also participated in eYantra Teacher's Robotic Competition 2015 and got 9th rank in North Region.

The club welcomes all those with a spark, who crave to dig into this vast promising realm, and ensures an all time eloquent mentorship. It has also got an online discussion forum to provide a common learning platform.

OBJECTIVE:
1.To apply their theoretical learning to real world situations.
2.To provide a platform where ideas can be shapened up.
3.To develop sense of practical learning.
4.To emerge as a one of the pioneer club in Embedded Systems & Robotics in the region/country.

VISION
"Engineering needs to dealt with a practical approach".
