ABOUT US

Research and Analysis Club is a club of ABES Engineering College, that operates under Dr. Ajay Agarwal, Chairman clubs ABESEC. It was formed in 2013 with a vision to provide a platform for students to present their technical as well as non-technical skills. The club has been organizing events related to technology as well as beyond technology to explore different edges of the world. The club always try to ensure that the events imbibe knowledge and can start a deep thinking process among the students. The club has earlier organized events like MEGAMIND'13, AAVISHKAR'13, IET Presentation Screening rounds, ITIHAAS'14, MYTHOS'14,Swacha Bharat Jagrukta Abhiyaan. Megamind'13, was the first event of the club, which was based on case study.Aavishkar'13 was an idea presentation event. The club also organized IET Presentation Screening rounds 2014, which led the college finalists to the next level of the event, in which one our college finalist secured 4th position in Delhi NCR. The club conducted its next event Itihaas'14, an online article presentation event during the summer vacations. Research and analysis club further organized a two day inter-college presentation event Mythos'14 on October 10-11, 2014.Aim of organizing this activity was to provide a platform, where students can show their presentation skills and compete with other students. The event immensely focused on unveiling the reality behind the mysteries and breaking the myths around the world. The club is currently under guidance of club coordinator, Mr. Pankaj Sharma (Asst. Prof, IT deptt.).
