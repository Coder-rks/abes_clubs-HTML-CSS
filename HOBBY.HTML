ABOUT US


Hobby Club is one of the most active clubs of ABES Engineering College. Its main aim is to organize events which highlight hobbies and interests of students. The club functions in four parts which divide the club in four different wings, namely, Research and Information Wing, Activity Co-ordination Wing, Publicity Wing and Discipline Wing. Research and Information Wing collects the interest and different ideas of students which helps to identify which kind of event is required to be organized. After finalizing of the event, Activity and Co-ordination Wing plays its part in managing the event in all respects. For an event to come in limelight, it needs publicity. So here comes the part of Publicity Wing where club advertises the event. Lastly, in any event we need discipline that should be maintained, so Discipline Wing plays its part. Hobby Club has been making college a beautiful place with its creativeness produced from events in forms such as Wall paintings, Collage made on college theme, Dustbins designed under Swachh Bharat Abhiyan and many more. Club tries to include all hobbies that a student can have and organize something that is different from academics. Students need to shape their hobbies and interests for personality development, which is one of the most important part of life. Students had been taking active part in such events organized by hobby club and it is expected the same in future.
