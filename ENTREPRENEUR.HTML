ABOUT US

Entrepreneurship has been the most important aspect of economic development and growth in the last period of business history. It means challenging the unknown and continuously creating the Future. Worldwide, these heroes of the modern economy changed the business environment and their companies play an increasingly important role in the global economy. They are like the growth engines of the economy.

Entrepreneurs are the ones who not only think of innovative ideas but also have the courage to implement it. But young students are needed to be promoted for becoming an entrepreneur. And our college is working a lot in this field. Our college promotes the students to think new and innovative ideas and help the good ones to be implemented or promoted.

There are many programs in our college which work towards promoting young entrepreneurs like"SPARK","SEEP" programs .These programs help to unleash the potential of young students and help them in their idea generation, validation and implementation.SEEP program helps in encouraging students to think innovatively and out of the box .It promotes the students to become job creators instead of job seekers. Besides all this students in our college manage and run different clubs like EClub, etc. This helps students to learn managing skills, team work, coordination and learning by doing.
