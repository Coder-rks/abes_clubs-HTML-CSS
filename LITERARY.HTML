ABOUT US

With the mission of expanding literature and poetry to the students literary club was formed in 2013 by Dr AMRITA TYAGI, Ass. Prof. AS-HU dept.

In order to make all-round development around the society ,country's conditious,national issues.Literary club organises events that address deep sholy in the roots of national issues and country's constitution.

Club organises time to time many events inorder to increase the literature ability of the students and make them bold of confident. Debates are often organized in order to make students confident enough among public and emerge them as public figures.

Literature club rather than club ,always works cooperatively like a family of 28 members. Whole club is divided into 4 main categories i.e. Publicity of promotion, Discipline Committee,Artistic and decoration and organizing committee.

Presently under the mentorship of Ms. MOKSHI JURAL (club coordinator).Club is planning to organize many new events ahead in the college.
