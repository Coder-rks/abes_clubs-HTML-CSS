ABOUT US


In the contemporaneous age of technological advancement, there arises a need for a technical committee for intellectual vision and TECHNOVATION is one such committee of the Computer Science Department of ABES Engineering College. It Organises various events which helps in raising the confidence level of students, impacting technical knowledge and building professional attitude in them.


Technovation is the technical club of Computer Science Department, ABES college. It was formed in 2010 with a vision to provide a platform for students to present their technical skills. With time it has emerged into a fully operational group having a team of 45 students having expertise in various fields.


OUR AIM

It is a committee which looks forward to bring new technological ideas and methods which could be implemented for developing intellectualism among budding minds.
